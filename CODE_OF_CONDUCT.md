# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project. Be constructive,
assume good faith, respect privacy and consent, and focus criticism on the work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats,
doxxing, publishing credentials or private data, and using voice-cloning
features without the speaker's consent. Maintainers may edit or remove content
and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact method in `SECURITY.md`.
